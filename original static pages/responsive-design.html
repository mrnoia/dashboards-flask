Bootstrap 5 Breakpoints:
  • xs - Extra small (0px and up) - Default
  • sm - Small (576px and up) - Landscape phones
  • md - Medium (768px and up) - Tablets
  • lg - Large (992px and up) - Desktops
  • xl - Extra large (1200px and up) - Large desktops
  • xxl - Extra extra large (1400px and up) - Extra large desktops
Responsive Class Naming:
  • property-breakpoint-value - Format for responsive classes
  • col-md-6 - 6 columns on medium screens and up
  • d-lg-none - Hide on large screens and up
  • text-sm-center - Center text on small screens and up
